Confirmed Speakers

Diversity statement. We are proud to present a diverse range of speakers, including experts from both academia and industry. Recognizing the importance of gender balance, we have made a conscious effort to ensure that our speakers include both male and female representatives. In addition, we have invited speakers from diverse geographical locations, from institutions across the United States as well as those in Southeast Asia. In recognition of the fact that foundation models are trained on the vast expanse of the internet, and thus inherently shaped by and impactful to each one of us, our workshop's emphasis on affiliation, gender, and geographical diversity reflects this all-encompassing spirit, fostering a truly representative dialogue on the future of robotics.
